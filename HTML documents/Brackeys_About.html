Asbjørn Thirslund

Asbjørn Thirslund (born: 1997), better known online as Brackeys,
is a video game developer and YouTuber that created tutorials related to video game programming
and development, usually using the Unity game engine.
He also hosts events called game jams,
where he and other developers have to design and create a small video game over the course of a few days;
at the end of the jam, the developers will play each others' games and declare a winner.
